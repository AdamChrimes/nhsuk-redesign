Your NHS pregnancy journey

Antenatal care is the care you get from health professionals during your pregnancy. You'll be offered 7 to 10 appointments with a midwife, or sometimes a doctor.

Signs and symptoms of pregnancy

Early signs of pregnancy can include a missed period, peeing more and feeling sick. Every woman is different and not all women will notice all of these symptoms.

Finding out and telling people

When you find out you're pregnant, you may feel happy and excited, or shocked, confused and upset. Everybody is different, and don't worry if you're not feeling as happy as you expected.
