2. Does it work for everyone?

The NHS is for everyone. Make sure people with different physical, mental health, social, cultural or learning needs can use your solutions.

Define the health, wellbeing or other measurable outcomes that your work will have an effect on. Measure them so you can tell how effective you have been.
