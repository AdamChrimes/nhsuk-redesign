3. Have you defined outcomes and measured success?

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Why it's important

If you don't know what good looks like, you won't know if the service is solving the problem it's meant to solve. By being clear about the outcomes early on, and measuring towards them, you decrease the risk of wasting NHS money doing work that doesn't achieve its intent.

What you must do

  • Define the outcomes your work will impact
  • List the top metrics which will indicate how well the service is solving the problem it's meant to
  • Track performance against the metrics you set
  • Show you've made decisions based on performance data to improve the service

What you may want to do

  • Openly publish your performance data
