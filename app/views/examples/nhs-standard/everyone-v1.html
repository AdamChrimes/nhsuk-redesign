2. Does it work for everyone?

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Why it's important

NHS services must work for everyone who needs to use them. And inclusive, accessible services are better for everyone. For example, simple language and clear typography is better for someone in a hurry as well as someone with a visual disability.

What you must do

  • Meet accessibility standards, like WCAG2.1
  • Avoid excluding any groups within the audience you serve

What you may want to do

  • Regularly carry out research with people with access needs
  • Make sure you don't excluse people who don't have digital skills or internet access.
