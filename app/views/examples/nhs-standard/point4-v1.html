Are you using the right tools and technologies?

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Why it's important

Decisions about technology tend to be a significant investment. Getting it right means you can build and run a good service in a cost effective way. The choices you make will have a huge impact on your ability to build, iterate and operate the service in a sustainable and reliable way.

What you must do

  • Articulate how the tools and technologies you choose allow you to build and operate a service in a cost effective way, and how they compare to alternatives
  • Calculate the total cost of ownership and keep it to a minimum
  • Explain how you've avoided getting locked into contracts for specific tools and suppliers, using open standards whenever possible
  • Maximise uptime and speed of responsible for any online parts of a service
  • Articulate how you control and manage any data you store
  • Have a plan for protecting confidential and sensitive information about users

What you may want to do

  • Use value chain mapping to show you've made good decisions [link]
  • Be able to deploy software changes regularly, without significant downtime
