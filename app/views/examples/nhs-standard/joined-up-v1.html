4. Are you providing a joined up experience?

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Why it's important

Fragmented services are difficult for users. Parts of a service need to come together into an obvious journey that makes sense to users, irrespective of the organisation, department or team that's responsible.

What you must do

  • Explain how the thing you are working on will become part of a wider service that solves a whole problem for users
  • If other organisations or teams work on other parts of the service, show a plan for working with them and iterating towards the wider service

What you may want to do

  • Publish things to raise awareness of what you are doing outside your team and organisation, increasing the potential for collaboration
  • Involve front line staff and policy people
