Why it's important

Lots of products and services aren't useful for users. Others are frustrating and hard to use. By developing a deep understanding of your users and their needs, you are more likely to help them achieve their goals.

What you must do

  • Be clear who your users are
  • Describe the problem users need you to solve
  • List the top user needs for each kind of user
  • Explain how your chosen solution meets your users' needs
  • Have evidence that these user needs are real
  • Have evidence that your solution works well for users

What you may want to do

  • Test frequently and improve/li>
  • Use consistent NHS.UK design patterns
  • Follow the NHS.UK content style guide
  • Use agile ways of working to reduce the risk of delivering the wrong thing
